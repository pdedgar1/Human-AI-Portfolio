Spring whispers through the frost-laden air / I step out / the world still wrapped in the quiet of dawn / my breath mingles with the mist / my dog at my side / paws padding on the dew-kissed ground / the street littered with yesterday’s party debris / discarded cups and wrappers / echoes of laughter and dog droppings left behind / the sky blushes with the first light / trees stretch their limbs / shaking off winter’s slumber / each step a conversation with the earth / the cold nips at my cheeks / a reminder of the night’s chill / yet the sun’s first rays warm my skin / my dog sniffs the air / sensing the subtle shift / the world begins to wake / but for now / it is ours alone / a sacred silence / a breath shared between us / man and dog / heartbeats in sync / walking the edge of night and day / spring’s gentle arrival / a symphony of stillness / a dance of life unfolding / quiet / beautiful / in the early morning light

Frost-laden air / cups and wrappers, trees stretch / night’s chill, first rays
